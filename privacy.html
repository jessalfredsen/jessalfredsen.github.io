Privacy Policy for DataGeneration.ai ApS

Effective Date: 1. Feb. 2024

At DataGeneration.ai ApS, your privacy is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our services.

1. Data Collection

We collect data strictly for analytical purposes. This includes:

  • Aggregated usage metrics
  • Anonymized behavioral patterns
  • Data points necessary for improving our services

We do not intentionally collect or store any information that can directly identify you as an individual unless explicitly consented to for specific cases.

2. Commitment to Anonymity

To ensure privacy:

  • We anonymize all collected data at the earliest possible stage.
  • We make it a priority to avoid retaining any single data point that can be traced back to a specific individual.
  • We implement robust data aggregation techniques to ensure that any output of our analyses cannot be de-anonymized or linked to a person.

3. Data Usage

The data we collect is used to:

  • Enhance and optimize the performance of our services.
  • Develop insights that improve user experience and overall value for our clients.
  • Drive innovation through aggregate-level trend analysis.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Anonymized data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for analytical purposes.
  • Data that cannot be anonymized or aggregated is not stored.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share data with third parties in a way that could identify individuals. Data may be shared in aggregated and anonymized formats only.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security protocols to protect the data we collect and process. These measures include:

  • Encryption during data transmission and storage.
  • Regular audits of our data handling processes.
  • Compliance with all relevant data protection regulations.

7. Your Rights

As part of our commitment to transparency and privacy:

  • You can request that any personal information inadvertently collected is deleted or anonymized immediately.
  • You may inquire about the nature of the data collected and processed by contacting us.

8. Updates to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of significant changes and ensure continued compliance with applicable laws and industry standards.
